Directive:

There are many poems that everyone learns throughout school, from grade school to college. We are given certain(and seemingly random) poems and most go in one ear and out the other but some stay with us. They stick in our minds, either because the cadence and repetition activate the obssesive part of our brain that keeps most song lyrics we'd like to forget on a constant loop in the back of our minds. Or because the poem made a connection with something we didn't know we couldn't put into words, but then there they are, on paper, said by someone else, your thoughts, feelings, hopes, dreams. Some of the these poems will be ones we've all had to learn and some will be ones we should all learn. Lets hope one or two stick in your mind for that second reason!
